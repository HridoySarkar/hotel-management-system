Meet the Team

Mirko Picicco
Managing Director

I am thrilled to welcome you to our website!

With more than a decade of experience, Mirko Picicco is an esteemed professional in the hospitality industry. After compleating his Bachelor's Degree in Hotel & Restaurant Management from the University of I.P.S.S.E.O.A. Amerigo Vespucci in Rome, he began his career with Illy Cafe, quickly rising to Manager before being sought after by Club Corp and Hilton Hotel

In 2016, he joined Investartone and has been successfully leading the hospitality division ever since. Throughout his Career, Mirko has held senior management and executive-level positions within the industry, rapidly becoming a key asset. He is regularly sought after for his recruiting and Human Resources workshops in London, and his insight, as well as his contributions to the hospitality sector, are invaluable.

Mirko is a dedicated professional with an impressive track record of success in the hospitality industry. He has a genuine passion for the sector, and is the committed to providing the highest quality of service to each and every client. His unwavering dedication to the industry is sure to make him a leading force in the sector for many years to come.

Filippo Becchino

Recruitment Consultant

His history in the world of restaurants was born and consolidated in Italy, prestigious and starred restaurants (such as: Restaurant Managers or supervisor) cover various roles.

His search for professional growth led him to London in 2018, where he held the role of Restaurant Floor Manager at the Baglioni Luxury Hotel in London for over three years.

The desire to start new experiences led him to meet Mirko, being able to put the great experience gained to good use.

Today his skills are available to MH Premier Staffing Solutions clients. As a recruiter, he can evaluate profiles with a vision that is not born on the benches of a university, but from managerial and operational experiences in medium-high-level restaurants.

His outstanding professionalism and the human component in managing staff resources are the characteristics that distinguish his profile.

Manila Bartocci

Recruitment Consultant

Being a professional Recruitment Consultant comes naturally to Manila. Experiencing her Grandfather's vineyard in childhood and developing a passion for Oenology, she further discovered her passion for fine dining while employed at various acknowledged Michelin establishments throughout Italy. A further opportunity would eventually bring Manilla to England where she helped develop a trademark Winebar/Restaurant and put it firmly on the map. With so much accomplished, she went on to successfully establish The Empire Bar and Restaurant including running theatre bars. Her varied experience and deep understanding of all aspects of the catering world have been honed by a life dedicated to the industry.
